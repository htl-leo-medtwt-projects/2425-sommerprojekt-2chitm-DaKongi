Learning

Learning new techniques is the first step to improving your solve times and getting better at cubing. Check out the Tutorials section, pick a method, and start mastering it!

Practice

You won't become great in a single day, even if you learn the most advanced techniques and use the best cube available. The key is practice—there's no such thing as too much of it!

Once you've reached a solid time, start competing against others—this will keep you motivated, challenge your skills, and push you to get even faster!
