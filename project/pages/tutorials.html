The LP (Layer Permutation) Method is a simple yet effective approach to solving the 2x2 Rubik's Cube. It's easy to learn, and with a bit of practice, you'll be able to solve the cube in under a minute!



If you're already proficient at solving a 3x3 quickly, you can apply the same method to a 2x2 while simply ignoring the center layers, as they don't exist. This approach is likely faster and eliminates the need to learn an entirely new method.
